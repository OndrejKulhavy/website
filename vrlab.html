Healthcare VR 3D Modeling Full Stack

VRLab is a specialized web application and database system designed to bridge the gap between medical imaging and surgical reality. It manages patient data and 3D anatomical models, distributing them directly to VR devices for pre-operative planning.

The system is currently used on a daily basis during transplant procedures at IKEM, allowing surgeons to preview and interact with patient-specific 3D models in the operating room. This project has grown from a final year project into a critical tool for clinical and experimental medicine.
